# Goals Playing

A study tracking app for building consistent progress toward personal goals.

---

## How Can the App Help People Reach a Goal?

Goals Playing helps people build the discipline needed to reach their goals by turning study time into a clear daily practice.

Instead of focusing only on the final result, the app helps users track small, consistent actions: studying a topic, meeting a daily goal, and maintaining a streak over time.
